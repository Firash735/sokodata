About this product

The story behind
Kenyan from Kenya.

Kenya is the world's largest exporter of black tea. The highlands of Kericho, Nandi, and Bomet receive over 1,400mm of rain annually, spread evenly across the year — creating conditions for continuous flush and year-round production. Kenyan tea is known for its bright coppery colour, brisk astringency, and exceptional strength in the cup.

IMPORTANT FOR BUYERS
Tea export from Kenya is regulated by the Tea Board of Kenya (TBK). Most smallholder tea is compulsorily purchased by KTDA factories. Private tea estate owners and some approved agents can export directly. Contact your MuftahX seller to confirm their export license status.
