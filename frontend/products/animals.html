About this product

The story behind
Kenyan from Kenya.

Kenya's live animal export sector has grown 767% in three years — the fastest-growing export category in the entire country. Driven by Gulf demand for Eid al-Adha and Eid al-Fitr, Kenyan cattle, goats, sheep, and camels are shipped live to Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, and Bahrain. The animals are Halal-certified, disease-free, and verified by Kenya Veterinary Authority.

IMPORTANT FOR BUYERS
Eid al-Adha demand drives 60-70% of annual live animal exports. Orders must be placed and logistics confirmed 8-10 weeks before Eid. Missing the Eid window means waiting a full year for the next peak demand cycle. Check the Islamic calendar and plan your inventory accordingly.
Compliance & certifications

What you need
to export legally.

Every certification requirement for kenyan live animals export — by destination market. Missing any of these will result in shipment rejection at destination.

🐄
KVA Health Certificate REQUIRED
Kenya Veterinary Authority health certificate per consignment. Includes vaccination records and disease-free certification.
🌟
Halal Certification GULF REQUIRED
NAAH or internationally recognized Halal body certification. Without this, no Gulf market entry for live animals or meat.
🌏
IGAD Regional Transit TRANSIT
Intergovernmental Authority on Development livestock transit documentation for cross-border movement within East Africa.
✈️
IATA Live Animals Regs AIR TRANSPORT
IATA Live Animals Regulations compliance for air transport. Sea transport requires CITES and IMO documentation.
