About this product

The story behind
Kenyan from Kenya.

Murang'a, Meru, and Nakuru counties produce Kenya's finest Hass avocados at 1,400–1,800 metres on the slopes of the Aberdare range and Mount Kenya. The cool nights, well-drained volcanic soil, and moderate rainfall create fruit of exceptional oil content — 18–22% — which produces the creamy, nutty flavour that European and Gulf consumers demand.

KEPHIS Certificate REQUIRED
Phytosanitary certificate per consignment. Fruit inspected at packhouse by KEPHIS officer before export.

GlobalGAP EU REQUIRED
Good Agricultural Practices. Required by all major EU supermarket buyers. Annual farm and packhouse audit.

Horticultural Crops Directorate export license. Annual registration for all horticultural exporters.
