Compliance first

No documents.
No listing. Full stop.

MuftahX is built on one non-negotiable principle: every seller is verified before any buyer sees them. This protects buyers, protects compliant exporters, and protects Kenya’s export reputation globally.

🌿
KEPHIS Certificate REQUIRED
Kenya Plant Health Inspectorate Service. Mandatory for all plant products. Covers phytosanitary standards for EU, US, and Gulf markets.
✅
GlobalGAP EU REQUIRED
Good Agricultural Practices demanded by EU supermarkets. Covers food safety, traceability, and environmental practices on-farm.
🇺🇸
FDA Registration US REQUIRED
Required for all food and agricultural exports to the United States under the Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA).
🏗
AFA / HCD License KE REQUIRED
Agriculture and Food Authority, Horticultural Crops Directorate. Required for all horticultural exporters operating in Kenya.
