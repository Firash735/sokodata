🌿
KEPHIS Certificate REQUIRED ALL
Kenya Plant Health Inspectorate Service. Mandatory for all plant products. Covers phytosanitary standards for EU, US, and Gulf markets. Issued per consignment at the Nairobi dry mill or packhouse.
✅
GlobalGAP EU REQUIRED
Good Agricultural Practices demanded by EU supermarkets. Annual farm audit. Covers food safety, traceability, worker welfare, and environmental practices on-farm. Without this, EU supermarkets will not list you.
🇺🇸
FDA Registration US REQUIRED
Required for all food and agricultural exports to the United States under FSMA. Online registration takes 2–4 weeks. Free to register. Without it, US customs will reject the shipment.
🏗
AFA / HCD License KE REQUIRED
Agriculture and Food Authority, Horticultural Crops Directorate. Annual license for all Kenyan horticultural exporters. Required before KEPHIS will issue certificates.
