## Step 1 — WhatsApp outreach

"Hi [Name], I run MuftahX — connecting Kenyan exporters
to buyers in the EU, UAE, and US. I'd like to list your

## Step 2 — Collect via WhatsApp

Request as photo or PDF:
- KEPHIS certificate (mandatory for plant products)
- AFA/HCD license (mandatory for horticulture)
- GlobalGAP certificate (EU buyers require it)
- FDA registration (US exports only)
